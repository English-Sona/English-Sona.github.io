Department of Humanities and Languages

One of the founding departments of Sona College of Technology, the Department of Humanities and Languages came into existence along with the establishment of the college in 1997. The main objective of the department is to develop communicative competence and performance of the students in English. A team of well experienced and vibrant English faculty with proven excellence in teaching and research carries forward the mission of the institution.
